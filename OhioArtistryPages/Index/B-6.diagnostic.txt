		<h3><a title="Jennifer Brown" href="https://oovar.ohioartscouncil.org/name/jennifer-brown#top/"><span class="fn n notranslate"><span class="given-name">Jennifer</span> <span class="family-name">Brown</span></span></a>

	<div class="cn-circled-excerpt">
		<p>
			</p><div class="cn-excerpt"><p>My current artistic practice is focused on printmaking and ceramics.&nbsp; I am a member artist at Studios on High Gallery in Columbus, Ohio, where I exhibit in both mediums.&nbsp; In the printmaking realm, I am most drawn to dry point etchings on acrylic and enjoy combining them with other techniques.&nbsp; I also create photopolymer gravure prints using light-sensitive metal plates (developed in plain water) and my own photographs and original drawings and combining these prints with graphite and charcoal. &nbsp;I am also a printmaking instructor at the Columbus Cultural Arts Center. &nbsp;The instructors and students all use non-toxic methods and materials for printmaking, and we are all conscious of reusing supplies and conserving our materials to the greatest extent possible. I ink and print all of my images by hand in small editions.&nbsp;</p>
